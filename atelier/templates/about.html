About

Atelier is a project that started on August of 2016 with the goal to give a better open source solution on 3DPrinting for printer hosts. Inspired by the maker spirit we started to build this new solution. With a modern interface, security and reliability.

With the help of KDE Community we are on the road to give you the best experience on using a printer host, either for printing or maintance.
